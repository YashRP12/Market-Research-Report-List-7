<p><strong>The Marine Silencers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive</li><li>Reactive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine silencers market can be categorized into two main types: resistive and reactive silencers. Resistive silencers work by dissipating sound energy through materials that absorb sound waves, effectively reducing noise levels. In contrast, reactive silencers focus on altering sound wave behavior through physical design, such as using chambers or bends, to cancel out specific noise frequencies. Both types are essential for ensuring compliance with noise regulations and enhancing operational comfort on marine vessels, while addressing different acoustic challenges.</p></p>
