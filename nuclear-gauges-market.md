<p><p>Nuclear gauges are instrumental in measuring various physical properties using radioactive isotopes. The market can be categorized into fixed and portable types. Fixed nuclear gauges are installed permanently at specific sites, such as construction or industrial facilities, providing continuous monitoring for parameters like density and moisture content. In contrast, portable nuclear gauges are designed for mobility, enabling use at multiple locations for on-site assessments, often in construction, geology, and environmental testing. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in measurement applications.</p></p>
