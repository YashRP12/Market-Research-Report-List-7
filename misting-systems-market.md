<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Systems</li><li>High Pressure Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Misting systems are categorized into low-pressure and high-pressure systems based on their operational mechanics. Low-pressure systems typically operate at around 10 to 50 psi, producing larger droplets that provide effective cooling and humidity control in residential and small commercial settings. In contrast, high-pressure systems operate at 800 to 1000 psi, generating a fine mist that evaporates quickly, offering efficient cooling in larger spaces like industrial settings, outdoor venues, and greenhouses. Each system serves distinct applications and operational requirements.</p></p>
