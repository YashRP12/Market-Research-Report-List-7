<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Blow Molding</li><li>Extrusion Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polycarbonate (PC) plastics market is segmented into three primary molding types: injection molding, blow molding, and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves injecting molten PC into molds to create complex shapes, ideal for high-volume production. Blow molding forms hollow objects by inflating a heated plastic tube, commonly used for bottles and containers. Extrusion molding involves forcing melted PC through a die to create continuous shapes, such as sheets and profiles, suitable for various industrial applications. Each method offers unique advantages based on the desired product and manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
