<p><ul><li>Oil-based Sealer</li><li>Water-based Sealer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt sealer market primarily consists of two types: oil-based sealers and water-based sealers. Oil-based sealers offer robust protection against UV rays and oxidation, making them suitable for high-traffic areas. They provide a glossy finish and longer-lasting results but can have environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based sealers are more environmentally friendly, with lower VOC levels and easier cleanup. They dry quickly and are less odorous, making them suitable for residential applications.</p></p>
