<p><strong>The Fiberglass Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chopped Strand Mat</li><li>Air Laid Mat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass mat market includes two primary types: chopped strand mat and air laid mat. Chopped strand mat is composed of randomly oriented strands of fiberglass, providing excellent strength and durability, commonly used in automotive and marine applications. In contrast, air laid mat is created by dispersing fiberglass strands in the air and bonding them, resulting in a lighter, more flexible material suited for applications like roofing and insulation. Both types cater to diverse industries, emphasizing performance and versatility.</p></p>
