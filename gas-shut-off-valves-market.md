<p><ul><li>Motorised Valve</li><li>Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas shut-off valves market comprises various types, primarily motorised valves and solenoid valves. Motorised valves utilize an electric actuator to control gas flow, offering automated and precise operations suitable for larger systems. In contrast, solenoid valves employ electromagnetic coils to open or close the valve, providing quick and efficient gas shut-off for smaller applications. Both types ensure safety and reliability in gas management, serving diverse industries such as oil and gas, HVAC, and manufacturing.</p></p>
