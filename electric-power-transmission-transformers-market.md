<p><p>The electric power transmission transformers market is categorized into three primary types: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage transformers. Low voltage transformers operate at voltages up to 1 kV, typically used for residential or commercial applications. Medium voltage transformers function between 1 kV and 36 kV, serving industrial and utility needs. High voltage transformers exceed 36 kV and are essential for long-distance power transmission and large-scale generation, ensuring efficient energy delivery across vast networks. Each type plays a critical role in maintaining grid stability and efficiency.</p></p>
