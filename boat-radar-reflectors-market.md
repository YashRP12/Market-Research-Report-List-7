<p><ul><li>Octahedral</li><li>Luneberg Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat radar reflectors enhance the visibility of vessels on radar systems, crucial for maritime safety. There are primarily two market types: octahedral and Luneburg lens reflectors. Octahedral reflectors consist of multiple flat, triangular facets that reflect radar signals from various angles, making them effective for smaller boats. Luneburg lens reflectors use a spherical design that provides a wide-angle return, offering increased detection range and clarity. Both types cater to different vessel sizes and operational needs, ensuring improved situational awareness at sea.</p></p>
