<p><strong>The Automotive Active Window Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combiner Technology</li><li>Windshield Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Active Window Display market encompasses two primary technologies: Combiner Technology and Windshield Technology. Combiner Technology utilizes a partially reflective surface to project information onto a small area of the windshield, enhancing driver visibility without obstructing the view. In contrast, Windshield Technology involves integrating a head-up display directly into the windshield glass, allowing for a larger and more immersive display area. Both technologies aim to improve driver awareness and safety by delivering crucial information in an easily accessible format.</p></p>
