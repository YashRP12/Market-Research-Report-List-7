<p><strong>The Avalanche Airbag Pack Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Canister Based Airbag</li><li>Fan Based Airbag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The avalanche airbag pack market is primarily categorized into two types: canister-based airbags and fan-based airbags. Canister-based airbags utilize a compressed gas canister to deploy the airbag rapidly, providing immediate buoyancy in an avalanche situation. In contrast, fan-based airbags rely on a battery-powered fan to inflate the airbag, offering a more sustainable solution but with potential weight and space considerations. Both types enhance safety for users in avalanche-prone areas by increasing visibility and reducing burial risk during avalanches.</p></p>
