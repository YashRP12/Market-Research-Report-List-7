<p><p>The aquarium lights market features several types: </p><p>1. **LED** lights are energy-efficient and long-lasting, offering customizable color options and low heat emission. </p><p>2. **Fluorescent** lights provide a broader spectrum and are suitable for planted aquariums, though less efficient than LEDs. </p><p>3. **Halogen** lights deliver bright illumination but produce more heat and consume more energy. </p><p>4. **Incandescent** lights, while inexpensive, are less commonly used due to lower efficiency and shorter lifespan. Each type serves different needs in aquatic environments.</p></p>
