<p><p>The Abrasive Polishing Fluid market is categorized into two main types: Mechanical Action Polishing Fluid and Chemical Action Polishing Fluid. Mechanical Action Polishing Fluid relies on abrasive particles to physically rub against surfaces, effectively removing imperfections and creating smooth finishes. In contrast, Chemical Action Polishing Fluid employs chemical agents to dissolve or alter the surface material at a molecular level, enhancing shine and clarity. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing and metalworking, for achieving high-quality surface finishes.</p></p>
