<p><p>The kitchen scales market comprises two main types: mechanical scales and digital scales. Mechanical scales utilize a spring mechanism to gauge weight, offering durability and simplicity without the need for batteries. They are often favored for their nostalgic design and reliability. In contrast, digital scales employ electronic sensors, providing precise measurements and often featuring additional functions like tare weight and units conversion. Digital scales are more popular among home cooks for their accuracy and ease of use in modern kitchens.</p></p>
