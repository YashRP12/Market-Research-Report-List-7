LED panels are a subset of LCDs but use light-emitting diodes for backlighting, enhancing brightness and efficiency. OLED panels offer superior picture quality with self-emissive technology, enabling deeper blacks and wider viewing angles. Each type serves diverse consumer preferences, driving advancements in display technology and enhancing viewing experiences across various applications.</p></p>
