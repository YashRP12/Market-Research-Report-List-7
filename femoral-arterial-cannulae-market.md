<p><p>The femoral arterial cannulae market is segmented into unipolar and bipolar types. Unipolar femoral arterial cannulae are designed for single-direction blood flow, typically used in minimally invasive procedures and for monitoring vascular access. In contrast, bipolar femoral arterial cannulae allow for bidirectional blood flow, enhancing functionality in more complex surgeries or treatments. Both types are essential in cardiopulmonary bypass and critical care settings, ensuring safe and effective cardiac support and perfusion management.</p></p>
