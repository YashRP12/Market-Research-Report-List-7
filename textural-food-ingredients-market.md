<p><p>The textural food ingredients market encompasses various categories that enhance the mouthfeel and stability of food products. Hydrocolloids, such as gelatin and pectin, act as thickeners and gelling agents, improving texture. Starch and its derivatives serve as binders and stabilizers, contributing to creaminess and viscosity. Emulsifiers, like lecithin, facilitate the blending of water and oils, ensuring a smooth consistency. Additional ingredients may include fibers and maltodextrins, which also play significant roles in texture enhancement and overall food quality.</p></p>
