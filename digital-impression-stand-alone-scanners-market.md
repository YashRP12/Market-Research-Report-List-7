<p><ul><li>Parallel Confocal Imaging</li><li>Optical Wand Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital impression stand-alone scanners utilize two prominent technologies: Parallel Confocal Imaging and Optical Wand Technology. Parallel Confocal Imaging captures precise 3D images by employing multiple imaging points, enhancing accuracy and speed in scans. This method allows for quick, detailed surface capturing ideal for dental and orthodontic applications. Conversely, Optical Wand Technology uses a handheld device to sequentially capture images with laser or light, providing a flexible, user-friendly scanning experience. Both technologies aim to streamline the impression process and improve patient outcomes in dental practices.</p></p>
