<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebulizers</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebulizers</li><li>Mesh Nebulizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable nebulizers market comprises three primary types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to aerosolize medication, making them effective but often bulky. Ultrasonic nebulizers employ high-frequency vibrations to create fine mist, offering quieter operation and compact design. Mesh nebulizers utilize a mesh membrane to generate aerosolized medication, providing rapid delivery and high portability. Each type caters to different patient needs, including convenience, speed of administration, and ease of use, driving market growth.</p></p>
