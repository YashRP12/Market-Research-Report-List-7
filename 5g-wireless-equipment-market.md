<p><p>The 5G wireless equipment market comprises several types of base stations. Macro Base Stations (Macro BS) provide extensive coverage over wide areas, ideal for urban environments. Micro Base Stations (Micro BS) serve smaller regions, enhancing capacity and coverage in densely populated areas. Pico Base Stations (Pico BS) focus on localized coverage in specific buildings or venues, while Femto Base Stations (Femto BS) cater to individual homes or small businesses, offering personalized connectivity solutions. Each type plays a crucial role in building a comprehensive 5G network.</p></p>
