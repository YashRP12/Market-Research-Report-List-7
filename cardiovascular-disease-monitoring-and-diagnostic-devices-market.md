<p><p>The cardiovascular disease monitoring and diagnostic devices market encompasses various technologies designed to assess heart health. ECG systems measure electrical activity, providing crucial diagnostic insights. Event monitors capture heart rhythms over extended periods, essential for detecting irregularities. Holter monitors offer continuous ECG recording for 24 to 48 hours, helping diagnose transient issues. Implantable loop recorders are used for long-term monitoring of cardiac events. ECG management systems streamline data analysis and reporting, enhancing clinical efficiency and patient care in cardiovascular diagnostics.</p></p>
