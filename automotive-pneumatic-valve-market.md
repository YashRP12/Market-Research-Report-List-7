<p><p>The automotive pneumatic valve market encompasses various valve types essential for vehicle operation. Engine valves regulate air-fuel intake and exhaust. Solenoid valves automate fluid or gas flow via electrical signals. Exhaust gas recirculation valves reduce emissions by recirculating exhaust gases. Tire valves maintain air pressure. Water valves control coolant flow. AT control valves manage automatic transmission fluid. Others include specialized valves for diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
