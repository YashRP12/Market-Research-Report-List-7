<p><strong>The Cylindrical Photoelectric Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diffuse Type Sensor</li><li>Diffusion Type Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cylindrical photoelectric sensors are essential in industrial automation, offering various types to suit specific applications. The diffuse type sensor detects objects directly by emitting light and measuring the reflection from the target, making it ideal for short-range detection. Conversely, the diffusion type sensor employs a reflective mechanism that utilizes light scattering, enabling detection of larger objects or varying surfaces over moderate distances. Both types contribute to enhanced precision and reliability in automation processes.</p></p>
