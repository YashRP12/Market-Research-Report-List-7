<p><p>The **Automotive Steering Column Market** includes **Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)** and **Electric Power Steering (EPS)** systems. HPS uses hydraulic fluid pressure to assist steering, offering a traditional, robust feel but with higher energy consumption. EPS relies on electric motors for assistance, providing better fuel efficiency, lighter weight, and adaptive features like lane-keeping. EPS is increasingly favored in modern vehicles due to its eco-friendliness and integration with advanced driver-assistance systems, while HPS remains prevalent in older or heavy-duty vehicles.</p></p>
