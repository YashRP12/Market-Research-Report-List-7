<p><p>The slurry valves market consists of two main types: wafer type and full flanged type. Wafer type valves are designed for easy installation between flanges, offering a compact and lightweight solution, making them ideal for space-constrained applications. Full flanged type valves, on the other hand, feature flanges on both ends, providing a robust connection suited for high-pressure systems. Both types cater to the needs of industries handling abrasive slurries, ensuring effective flow control and minimizing wear and tear.</p></p>
