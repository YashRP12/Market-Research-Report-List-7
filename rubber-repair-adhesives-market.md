<p><strong>The Rubber Repair Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Bond</li><li>Cold Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber repair adhesives market is divided into two main types: hot bond and cold bond adhesives. Hot bond adhesives require heat to activate and create a strong, durable bond, making them suitable for high-stress applications. Cold bond adhesives, on the other hand, cure at room temperature, offering a more user-friendly option with easier application and handling. Both types are effective for repairing rubber products, but their selection depends on the specific requirements of the repair, such as speed and strength.</p></p>
