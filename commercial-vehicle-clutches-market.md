<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Clutches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Clutches</li><li>Electromagnetic Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle clutches market includes friction and electromagnetic clutches. Friction clutches, the most common type, use friction plates to transfer power between the engine and transmission, ensuring smooth gear shifts and durability under heavy loads. Electromagnetic clutches, on the other hand, rely on magnetic fields to engage and disengage, offering precise control, reduced wear, and quieter operation. Both types cater to varying commercial vehicle needs, balancing performance, efficiency, and operational demands in diverse applications.</p></p>
