<p><p>Polyether ether ketone (PEEK) is a high-performance thermoplastic known for its strength, thermal resistance, and chemical stability. The market for PEEK is categorized into three main types: glass-filled, carbon-filled, and unfilled. Glass-filled PEEK incorporates glass fibers to enhance its mechanical strength and stiffness. Carbon-filled PEEK adds carbon fibers for improved rigidity and wear resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications. Unfilled PEEK offers excellent overall performance but is less reinforced, making it more versatile for a variety of applications.</p></p>
