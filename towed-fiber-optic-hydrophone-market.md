<p><p>The towed fiber optic hydrophone market comprises two main types: passive and active sensors. Passive sensors detect acoustic signals without emitting their own sound, making them ideal for surveillance and monitoring applications, particularly in underwater environments. In contrast, active sensors emit sound waves and analyze the returning echoes, allowing for object detection and identification. Both types leverage advanced fiber optic technology, enhancing sensitivity, reducing weight, and providing greater versatility for various military, commercial, and environmental applications.</p></p>
