Bluetooth technology provides short-range communication, ideal for personal and small-group interactions, with easy device pairing. Infrared technology utilizes line-of-sight for secure communication, suitable for environments requiring minimal interference. Radio technology offers long-range connectivity, making it ideal for outdoor and larger spaces, facilitating robust transmission. Each technology caters to specific user needs, enhancing communication efficiency across various applications and environments.</p></p>
