<p><p>The RF inductors market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Wire wound RF inductors utilize coiled wire to achieve higher inductance values, suitable for power applications. Film RF inductors are characterized by thin-film technologies offering low parasitic elements, ideal for high-frequency circuits. Multilayer RF inductors consist of multiple layers of conductive and insulating materials, providing compact designs with high inductance in small footprints. Each type serves distinct roles in improving the performance and efficiency of RF circuits.</p></p>
