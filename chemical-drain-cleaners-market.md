<p><p>The chemical drain cleaners market comprises primarily two types: alkaline and acidic. Alkaline cleaners contain caustic soda or sodium hydroxide, which effectively dissolve organic materials like hair and grease through saponification. Acidic cleaners, often based on sulfuric or hydrochloric acid, target mineral-based clogs such as scale and rust by breaking them down through a chemical reaction. Both types are popular for their quick action, but safety precautions are essential due to their corrosive nature.</p></p>
