<p><ul><li>Water Scrubber</li><li>Chemical Scrubber</li><li>PSA</li><li>Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biogas upgrading equipment enhances the quality of biogas for use as renewable energy. Water scrubbers use water to absorb impurities and elevate methane concentration. Chemical scrubbers deploy chemical solutions to react with contaminants, effectively cleaning the gas. Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) systems utilize pressure changes to separate methane from carbon dioxide and other gases. Membrane technology filters the gas through selective membranes, allowing methane to pass while blocking other gases, ensuring high-purity biogas production. Each method has distinct advantages in efficiency and cost.</p></p>
