<p><p>The syringe micropump market is segmented into single syringe pumps and dual syringe pumps. Single syringe pumps utilize one syringe to deliver precise fluid volumes, ideal for applications requiring low flow rates and straightforward operations. Dual syringe pumps feature two syringes, allowing for simultaneous dispensing of different fluids or precise mixing of substances, enhancing versatility in complex procedures. Both types are widely used in medical, pharmaceutical, and research settings for their accuracy, reliability, and capability to handle precise liquid control.</p></p>
