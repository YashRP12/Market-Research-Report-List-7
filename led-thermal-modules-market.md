<p><p>LED thermal modules encompass various types designed to manage heat dissipation in lighting applications. Heat sinks facilitate efficient thermal management through enhanced surface area. Ceramic PCBs provide excellent thermal conductivity and electrical insulation. Fansinks combine heatsink and fan functionalities for active cooling. Thermal clad boards serve as both substrates and heat spreaders, optimizing thermal performance. Thermally conductive pads enhance contact between components to promote heat transfer. Other emerging technologies continue to evolve, addressing diverse thermal management needs in LED systems.</p></p>
