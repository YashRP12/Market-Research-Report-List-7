<p><p>Textile Reinforced Concrete (TRC) is an advanced composite material that integrates high-strength textiles, such as alkali-resistant glass or carbon fibers, into concrete. This innovative technology enhances the mechanical properties of concrete, improving tensile strength and durability while reducing the overall weight of structures. TRC is gaining traction in construction applications, including facades, bridges, and rehabilitation projects due to its superior performance and sustainability.</p><p>The Textile Reinforced Concrete Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for lightweight and robust construction materials. Additionally, the rise in urbanization and the need for retrofitting aging structures are further propelling market expansion. Moreover, growing awareness of the benefits of TRC, such as reduced material consumption and improved resource efficiency, is leading to its broader adoption among architects and engineers.
