<p><ul><li>SMT Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary DIP switch market is categorized into two main types: SMT (Surface Mount Technology) and Through Hole type. SMT Rotary DIP switches are designed for surface mounting on PCBs, offering space-efficient solutions and automated assembly benefits. In contrast, Through Hole types are inserted into drilled holes on the PCB, providing robust connections and ease of replacement. Both types are crucial in various applications, including telecommunications, industrial controls, and consumer electronics, catering to different design and performance requirements.</p></p>
