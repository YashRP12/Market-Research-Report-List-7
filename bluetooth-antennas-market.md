<p><p>The Bluetooth antennas market is divided into two main types: external and internal antennas. External Bluetooth antennas are typically designed for applications requiring enhanced range and connectivity, often found in devices like routers and industrial equipment. They can be easily replaced or upgraded. In contrast, internal Bluetooth antennas are embedded within devices, providing a compact design suited for smartphones, laptops, and wearables. Internal antennas are optimized for space efficiency, making them ideal for consumer electronics and portable devices.</p></p>
