<p><strong>The Autotransfusion Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On Pump Transfusion Devices</li><li>Off Pump transfusion Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Autotransfusion devices facilitate the recovery and reinfusion of a patient's blood during surgery. The market is divided into two main types: on-pump and off-pump transfusion devices. On-pump devices are integrated with cardiopulmonary bypass systems, allowing for continuous blood flow management during procedures like open-heart surgery. In contrast, off-pump devices are used in surgeries where the heart remains beating, enabling blood collection and reinfusion without the need for bypass machinery, enhancing patient safety and recovery.</p></p>
