<p><p>The industrial girth gear market comprises primarily two types: spur and helical girth gears. Spur girth gears feature straight teeth and provide high efficiency and simple design, making them suitable for low-speed applications. In contrast, helical girth gears have angled teeth, allowing for smoother operation and higher load capacity, ideal for high-speed scenarios. Both types play critical roles in various industries, including mining, cement, and power generation, enhancing machinery performance and durability.</p></p>
