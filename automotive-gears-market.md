<p><p>The automotive gears market includes various types essential for vehicle performance. **Spur gears** are simple, cost-effective, and used in low-speed applications. **Helical gears** offer smoother, quieter operation due to angled teeth, ideal for high-speed transmissions. **Bevel gears** transfer motion between intersecting shafts, commonly in differential systems. **Worm gears** provide high torque reduction and are used in steering mechanisms. **Gear racks** convert rotational motion to linear, essential in steering systems. **Others** include specialized gears for unique applications, enhancing efficiency and functionality in automotive systems.</p></p>
