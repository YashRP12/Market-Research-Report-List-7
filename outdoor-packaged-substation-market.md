<p><p>The Outdoor Packaged Substation market primarily features two types of transformers: liquid-filled transformers and dry-type transformers. Liquid-filled transformers utilize oil as a coolant and insulator, offering excellent thermal performance and high voltage capacity, making them suitable for larger installations. Conversely, dry-type transformers use solid insulation and are generally more compact and safer in urban environments, as they pose reduced fire hazards. Each type serves specific applications, addressing various operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
