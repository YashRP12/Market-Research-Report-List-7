<p><p>Veno-Venous Extracorporeal Life Support (VV ECLS) devices are classified primarily into single-ventricular and biventricular categories. Biventricular devices, on the other hand, support both ventricles, encompassing more complex cases where both cardiac output and gas exchange need enhancement.
