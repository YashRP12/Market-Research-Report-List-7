<p><p>The liquid detergent chemicals market is segmented into four main types: Anionic, Cationic, Non-Ionic, and Zwitterionic detergents. Anionic detergents, known for their strong cleaning ability, are widely used in household and industrial applications. Cationic detergents possess antimicrobial properties, making them suitable for fabric softeners and disinfectants. Non-Ionic detergents are versatile and effective in various conditions, especially in sensitive applications. Zwitterionic detergents, with both positive and negative charges, offer unique properties for specialized cleaning tasks, appealing to niche markets.</p></p>
