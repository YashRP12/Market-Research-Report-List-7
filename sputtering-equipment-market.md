<p><p>The sputtering equipment market includes several distinct types based on their physical and chemical processes. Physics sputtering utilizes high-energy particles impacting a target material to eject atoms for deposition. Electronic sputtering focuses on thin film applications in electronics, mainly for semiconductor manufacturing. Etching involves removing material to create patterns or structures on substrates, crucial in microfabrication. Chemical sputtering combines chemical reactions with sputtering techniques to enhance material removal or deposition, offering versatile solutions for various industrial applications.</p></p>
