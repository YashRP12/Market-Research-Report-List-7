<p><strong>The LCD Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IPS Monitor</li><li>VA Monitor</li><li>PLS Monitor</li><li>AHVA Monitor</li><li>TN Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD monitor market features various technologies that cater to different user needs. IPS (In-Plane Switching) monitors offer wide viewing angles and accurate color reproduction, ideal for graphics work. VA (Vertical Alignment) monitors provide high contrast ratios, enhancing dark scenes. PLS (Plane to Line Switching) is Samsung's variant of IPS, known for affordability and good color performance. AHVA (Advanced Hyper Viewing Angle) is a proprietary technology offering better viewing angles. TN (Twisted Nematic) monitors are cost-effective with fast response times but limited color and angle performance.</p></p>
