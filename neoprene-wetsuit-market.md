<p><p>The neoprene wetsuit market is categorized into three main types: unlined, single lining, and double lining. Unlined wetsuits offer maximum flexibility and are ideal for warm water activities but lack insulation. Single lining wetsuits feature a soft inner layer for added warmth and comfort while maintaining flexibility. Double lining wetsuits have both exterior and interior layers, providing excellent thermal insulation and durability, making them suitable for colder conditions. Each type serves distinct preferences and environmental needs for water sports enthusiasts.</p></p>
