<p><p>The medical multimodal imaging market encompasses various technologies that integrate multiple imaging modalities for enhanced diagnostic capabilities. PET-CT combines positron emission tomography and computed tomography, providing functional and anatomical information. SPECT-CT merges single-photon emission computed tomography with CT for improved localization of diseases. PET-MR combines PET's functional imaging with magnetic resonance's detailed soft tissue visualization. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) offers high-resolution imaging of tissues, particularly in ophthalmology. Other market segments may include emerging technologies and hybrid systems, expanding diagnostic possibilities.</p></p>
