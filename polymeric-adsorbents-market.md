<p><p>The polymeric adsorbents market includes various types such as Aromatic, Modified Aromatic, Methacrylic, and Others. Aromatic adsorbents are based on polystyrene-divinylbenzene, offering high surface area and affinity for non-polar compounds. Modified Aromatic adsorbents are chemically altered to enhance selectivity for specific molecules. Methacrylic adsorbents, derived from methacrylate polymers, are ideal for polar compounds due to their hydrophilic nature. The "Others" category includes niche or hybrid adsorbents tailored for specialized applications. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs, such as purification, separation, and recovery processes.</p></p>
