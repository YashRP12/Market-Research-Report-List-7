<p><p>The curtain wall adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: acidic adhesive and neutral adhesive. Acidic adhesives contain an acid component that can enhance bonding strength but may corrode certain substrates over time. They are often used in environments where strong adhesion is crucial. On the other hand, neutral adhesives are formulated to be non-corrosive, making them suitable for a wider range of materials and applications. These options cater to different project requirements, ensuring optimal performance and longevity in construction.</p></p>
