<p><p>The stent delivery system market is primarily categorized into peripheral and vascular stent delivery systems. Peripheral stent delivery systems are designed for treating blockages in arteries outside the heart, such as those in the legs and arms, enhancing blood flow. In contrast, vascular stent delivery systems focus on coronary arteries within the heart, aiming to prevent restenosis and improve cardiac function. Both systems utilize advanced technologies for precise deployment, contributing to improved patient outcomes in managing vascular diseases.</p></p>
