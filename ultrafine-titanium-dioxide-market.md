<p><strong>The Ultrafine Titanium Dioxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anatase Based</li><li>Rutile Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrafine titanium dioxide market is primarily divided into two types: anatase-based and rutile-based titanium dioxide. Anatase-based titanium dioxide is known for its high specific surface area and is often used in applications requiring photocatalytic activity, such as air purification and self-cleaning coatings. Rutile-based titanium dioxide, on the other hand, boasts superior opacity and weather resistance, making it ideal for pigments in coatings, plastics, and paints. Both types serve distinct industrial applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
